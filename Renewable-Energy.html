Our Commitment to Renewable Energy

To save our environment and keep our world livable for future generations, we are committed to renewable energy. Since our factory's inception, we have taken initiatives to use clean energy sources. Currently, we are installing a new solar system with a capacity of 413.70KWp, aiming to generate electricity by early 2025. This will allow us to produce nearly 33% of our required power through solar energy, with plans to reach 100% solar coverage by the end of 2025.

  • 413.70KWp Solar System Installation
  •   33% Power from Solar by 2025
  • Goal: 100% Renewable Energy
