About

Barnali Textile & Printing Ind. (Pvt.) Ltd.

Barnali Textile is a 100% export oriented composite knitwear manufacturing industry that has been producing premium quality readymade knit garments for its valued clients in the EU market for over two decades. Our state-of-the-art latest technology in knitting, dyeing, cutting, printing, sewing and finishing facilities in one composite factory compound makes us capable to deliver any challenging design both in the knit fabrics and finished garments productions.

Our innovative R&D and relentless efforts in the Yarn-to-Fabric to finished knitwear production systems empower us to fast manufacture any volume and variety of knit garments by meeting the market demands and requirements of our esteemed buyers in Europe that lead to business successes at both ends. Our expertise and Performance has resulted to long lasting business relationships with our reputed clients in the EU market for decades.

Knitting

Our knitting department is equipped with advanced circular and flat knitting machines, enabling us to produce a wide range of high-quality knit fabrics. We specialize in various fabric structures and blends, ensuring flexibility and precision to meet diverse customer requirements for both fashion and functional garments.

Dyeing

Our dyeing facility utilizes state-of-the-art machines and eco-friendly processes to ensure vibrant, consistent, and high-quality colors on all types of knit fabrics. We follow strict quality control and sustainable practices, offering a wide range of color options while maintaining colorfastness and minimizing environmental impact.

Printing

Our printing division features advanced screen printing and digital printing technologies, capable of producing vibrant, durable prints on various knit fabrics. We offer precise color matching, intricate design reproduction, and eco-friendly printing solutions that meet international quality standards while maintaining excellent wash fastness and durability.
