Our Printing Technology

Types of Printing we offer

  • Pigment print
  • Rubber / Plastisol print
  • High density print
  • Sticker transfer
  • Foil print
  • Glitter print
  • Stone transfer
  • Photo / Puff print
  • Crack print

Dryer: Conveyor belt type for efficient drying.
