About Us

Barnali Collections Ltd is a 100% Export Oriented Knit Garments Industry located in Chittaranjan Textile Polli, Arambag, Godnail, Shiddhirganj, Narayanganj-1432, Bangladesh in a 4-storied modern building. It is situated on the banks of the Sitalakhya River and started its export operation in September 2021. It has been developed through its CEO and the dedicated staff under the name of Barnali Collections Ltd.

Barnali Collections Ltd is one of the leading manufacturers located in Narayanganj, Bangladesh, serving foreign buyers. We provide services to foreign buyers concerned with identifying products in the Bangladeshi market, negotiating prices and proper sourcing of products. We assure the highest degree of professionalism and provide a wide variety of services to foreign buyers. Our presence in the international market keeps us well-informed of customer tastes and changing market scenarios.

We offer fast and reliable delivery, excellent Quality Control and flexible manufacturing and production options. We have mastered producing fancy items. We have a unique methodology of paperwork that is immaculately followed and managed by our team. Our team consists of highly professional, skilled personnel trained to meet the increasing standards of the company. Our team consists of management, merchandisers, quality control, schedulers, and commercial personnel.

Company Strength

  • Strong Sourcing Network: With our extensive network of suppliers across Bangladesh, Barnali Collections Ltd is well positioned to select the right vendors for your valued orders, ensuring competitive pricing, quality and timely delivery.
  • Quality manual compliant to international codes and standards.
  • In-house expertise in initial sampling & pattern making.
  • Hi-fashion garments with a lot of value addition.
  • Varieties of prints: hand, machine & all-over.
  • Different kinds of washes and dyeing, antique look range, print options.
