Delegation Visit: Kuwait Armed Forces at Barnali Collections Ltd

A special visit by the Kuwait Armed Forces highlights international collaboration and showcases Barnali Collections Ltd's production excellence.

A delegation of eight members of the Kuwait Armed Forces visited Narayanganj. During this time, they visited two companies: Chittaranjan Textile Palli and Barnali Collections Ltd.

Visit Details

At 10:30 A.M on Tuesday (25 September 2023), the delegation, led by Major General Khaled AHHM Al-Qandari of the Kuwait Army, visited the two institutions.

Attendees

Bangladesh Army Brigadier General Shahidul Abedin Hasan, nine army officers and senior officials from Barnali Collections Ltd were present, along with the tour companions of the Kuwait Army.

Facility Tour

The visiting Kuwait Army delegation toured the production facilities of Barnali Collections Ltd. During this time, they met with the authority of the institution to exchange their views.

Conclusion

Later, the visiting party left Siddhirganj at 12 o' noon, marking the end of a successful and collaborative visit.

Barnali Textile & Printing Ind. (Pvt.) Ltd.

Barnali Textile is a leading 100% export-oriented composite knitwear manufacturer, delivering premium quality readymade knit garments to valued clients across the EU market for over two decades.
