Message from the Managing Director

Welcome to Barnali Textile, where excellence is woven into every thread. As a leading 100% export-oriented composite knitwear manufacturer, we have proudly delivered premium garments to our valued clients across the EU for over two decades. Our state-of-the-art facility integrates advanced technology in knitting, dyeing, cutting, printing, sewing and finishing, enabling us to fulfill diverse and challenging design requirements with precision and speed.

Through continuous innovation and dedicated R&D, we offer flexible, rapid production tailored to the evolving needs of our esteemed buyers. Our commitment to compliance, sustainability and employee welfare is reflected in our certifications, including ACCORD Green Category, BSCI, OEKO-TEX Standard 100, GOTS, OCS, RCS and LEED Gold. We nurture a safe, healthy and supportive workplace, providing medical care, subsidized meals and comprehensive welfare programs for our team.

At Barnali Textile, we believe in building win-win relationships with our clients and employees. Together, we create lasting partnerships and a thriving Barnali Textile family.
